About Us

Founded in 2024, SKAG's mission is to apply AI and DevOps to solve real-world problems in the veterinary industry. By leveraging AI for predictive health insights and DevOps for continuous automation, we aim to optimize pet care and streamline veterinary operations.

Our Project

AI-Driven Veterinary Platform

We developed an AI-powered platform that helps veterinarians with early disease detection, treatment optimization, and real-time health monitoring of pets and livestock. By combining AI and DevOps practices, we ensure scalability and automation in veterinary clinics.

Features

  • Predictive Analytics: Using AI models to predict potential health issues before they arise, ensuring timely interventions.
  • Automated Health Records: Automatically updates and analyzes health records to provide accurate and real-time insights.
  • Seamless Workflow Automation: DevOps practices to automate the deployment of updates and manage clinic resources efficiently.
  • Scalable Platform: Easily scales to serve both small veterinary clinics and larger animal care facilities.
